Description
Bundle of Joy is an instrumental piece from the 2015 Disney/Pixar animated film, Inside Out.

Growing up can be a bumpy road, and it's no exception for Riley, who is uprooted from her Midwest life when her father stars a new job in San Francisco. Like everyone else, Riley is guided by her emotions - Joy, Fear, Anger, Disgust, and Sadness. The emotions live in Headquarters, the control center inside Riley's mind, where they help advise her through everyday life.
